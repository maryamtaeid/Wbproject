Daily Calm Challenges

I created these daily challenges for you, starting from the weekend, to help you prepare for a stress-free week ahead. By following these small tasks throughout your workweek, you can enjoy a calmer, more balanced week.

Sat Sun Mon Tue Wed Thu Fri

Saturday — Phone‑Free Day

Unplug to reconnect with yourself and loved ones.

Sunday — Caffeine‑Free Day

Give your nervous system a gentle break.

Monday — 20‑Minute Walk

Step outside and soak up some serenity.

Tuesday — 10‑Minute Meditation

Breathe in calm, breathe out tension.

Wednesday — Gratitude Journal

Write down three things you’re thankful for.

Thursday — 5‑4‑3‑2‑1 Grounding

Anchor your mind in the present moment.

Friday — 30‑Minute Activity

Move your body; boost your mood.
